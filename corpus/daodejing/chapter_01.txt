道德经 — 第一章

道可道，非常道；
名可名，非常名。
无名天地之始；
有名万物之母。
故常无，欲以观其妙；
常有，欲以观其徼。
此两者，同出而异名，
同谓之玄。
玄之又玄，众妙之门。

--- Notes ---
Source: 道德经 / Tao Te Ching (Laozi)
Chapter: 1
Key theme: The named and the nameless; duality generating multiplicity
Mathematical note: The duality of 有/无 (being/non-being) mirrors the binary
alphabet {0,1} of the golden-mean shift. The constraint x²=x+1 generates all
structure from a single relation — "玄之又玄" (mystery upon mystery) resonates
with the recursive self-similarity of the modular tower X_∞ = lim X_m.
